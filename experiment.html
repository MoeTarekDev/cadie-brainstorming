Checkout Shipped

One-click checkout flow

Alex D.
Created Oct 24, 2023 Updated Oct 28, 2023

Context & Hypothesis

Currently, users drop off at the cart review step 42% of the time. We believe that by providing a single "Buy Now" button that utilizes saved payment methods and bypasses the cart entirely, we can increase conversion for returning users.

Artifacts

Figma Source Prototype Video
Screenshot or Figma Embed goes here Checkout Flow v3

Outcome & Learnings

Status changed to Testing

Oct 28, 2023

The one-click flow increased conversion by 14% for returning users. However, it caused a 5% increase in customer support tickets regarding accidental purchases.

Status changed to Shipped

Oct 29, 2023

Shipped to production. Added a 5-second "Undo" toast to mitigate accidental purchases.
